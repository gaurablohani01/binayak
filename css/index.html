Hello and Namaste! I am

Sujan Lohani

Master in Chemistry from Tribhuvan University

I completed my Master's in Chemistry from Tribhuvan University, Nepal. As a passionate chemist with an inquisitive mind, I'm eager to delve deep into the fascinating world of chemical sciences, with a special focus on analytical chemistry and materials characterization.

During my academic journey, I embarked on a path of exploration and discovery in the realm of chemistry. Engaging in several research projects and internships proved to be transformative experiences that shaped my scientific interests and career aspirations. These opportunities provided me with invaluable hands-on experience working alongside brilliant minds and academicians, exposing me to the exciting worlds of analytical chemistry, inorganic synthesis, and materials science.

When I'm not busy with research or studying, I love finding inspiration in the world around me. Nature has a way of captivating my senses, and I enjoy spending time outdoors, taking in the beauty of the environment. Observing the natural world reminds me of the intricate chemical processes that underpin all life and matter, fueling my curiosity to learn more about the molecular interactions that shape our everyday experiences.

My primary research interest revolves around chemical analysis and characterization techniques. I am deeply fascinated by how advanced analytical methods can unveil the hidden properties of matter and contribute to solving real-world problems. I'm also passionate about exploring sustainable chemical processes and green chemistry approaches, a cause that aligns with my commitment to environmental protection and eco-friendly technologies.
